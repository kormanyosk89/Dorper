Who we are

For more than a decade, I have been involved in sheep farming. I first encountered the Dorper breed during my university years under the professional guidance of Dr. János Oláh.

Since then, I have been building the flock through conscious and consistent breeding work. The herd currently consists of around 100 animals and is located in Békés county, near Kaszaper.

What I recognized in the Dorper is what is becoming increasingly important in European conditions: adaptability, resilience, and cost-effective keeping.
